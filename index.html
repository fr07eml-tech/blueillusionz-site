Case studies in execution, transformation, and measurable impact.

Selected examples of programs where strategy, operations, technology, and stakeholder alignment had to move together. The first two have been reframed for the current portfolio, program, and growth strategy; the remaining case studies are retained for expansion in the next iteration.

Department of State · CARE Program

Stabilizing execution and strengthening governance in a $9B+ federal program environment.

Challenge: The program operated within a complex, multi-agency environment supporting global relocation efforts and required stronger governance, coordination, and execution discipline.

Action: Led execution-focused initiatives to improve delivery alignment, standardize processes, and strengthen oversight across operational, technical, and leadership stakeholders.

  • Established PMO governance frameworks, reporting rhythms, and accountability structures.
  • Supported acquisition and contract oversight within a $9B+ federal program environment.
  • Improved coordination and decision visibility across agencies and stakeholder groups.
  • Outcome: Improved execution consistency, reporting visibility, and operational scalability.
DHS · Transportation Security Administration

Driving nationwide operational performance improvement across TSA.

Challenge: TSA required improved operational efficiency and effectiveness across airport security operations while maintaining mission readiness and compliance at scale.

Action: Led nationwide process improvement initiatives using Lean Six Sigma methodologies and aligned operational procedures with workforce performance and training needs.

  • Implemented process optimization strategies across airport operations.
  • Partnered with TSA leadership and FLETC to integrate process improvements into training and operations.
  • Outcome: Increased passenger throughput and detection effectiveness by 70%.
  • Strengthened workforce capability, operational consistency, and mission readiness at scale.
U.S. Navy · Modernization

Consolidating legacy systems and improving enterprise performance.

Led delivery of modernization initiatives including the Navy PCS Mobile App and N1 55-to-1 system consolidation.

  • Reduced redundancy by more than 60% through consolidation of 55 legacy systems into a unified platform.
  • Improved operational efficiency, user experience, and governance across enterprise systems.
  • Aligned modernization delivery with mission readiness and global mobility requirements.
GAO · Knowledge Services

Modernizing reporting systems for accuracy, visibility, and efficiency.

Led transformation of GAO reporting systems supporting congressional and international stakeholders.

  • Improved data accuracy from 45% to 98% and strengthened program reporting and decision support.
  • Delivered $400K+ in annual savings through process redesign and system improvements.
  • Recognized by the Comptroller General for improving accessibility, reporting quality, and executive decision-making.
FDIC & NRC · PMO / Continuity

Supporting crisis operations and continuity through governance discipline.

Led operational support and PMO governance in highly regulated federal environments.

  • Led operational support for 192 FDIC financial institution closures during large-scale federal response operations.
  • Established PMO governance frameworks aligned with ITIL and continuity of operations standards.
  • Improved execution, reporting, risk management, and stakeholder coordination.
DoD CIO / IRS · Portfolio Optimization

Improving governance, reporting, and performance through modernization.
